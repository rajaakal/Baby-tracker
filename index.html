💡 7-Month Tips
🌙
Sleep goal: 7-month-olds typically need 12–16 hours of total sleep per day, including 2–3 naps.
🍼
Feeding: At 7 months, breast milk or formula remains primary. Offer 3–5 nursing/bottle sessions plus beginning solids 1–2 times/day.
⏰
Wake windows: Your baby can typically stay awake for 2.5–3 hours between naps before getting overtired.
🥣
Solids: Start with purees — single-ingredient foods like sweet potato, avocado, or pea. Wait 3 days between new foods to watch for reactions.
💧
Hydration: A small amount of water (2–4 oz/day) can be introduced with solids at 6+ months. Breast milk/formula is still the main source.
📱
Track patterns: Look for your baby's natural schedule. Most 7-month-olds settle into a 2-nap routine around this age.
